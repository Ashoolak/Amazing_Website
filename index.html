KPMG Ideation Challenge

I am excited to share that after winning the sectional and regional stages, last Friday my teammates and I represented Metro Vancouver in the national stage of the KPMG Ideation Challenge. Our idea, which addressed the mental health challenges faced by miners, involved leveraging Meta, VR, and AI to create a platform called P.A.A.T that supplements current physical training.

UBC Biztech Innovent

Together with my talented teammates and I competed in the sustainability stream and developed a cutting-edge prototype robot that can autonomously clean ship hulls. The robot was equipped with two Ultrasonic and Infrared sensors as well as two propellers which were all programmed using an Arduino. The competition was an excellent opportunity to network with like-minded individuals and industry experts and I am proud to announce that we placed second in our stream!
